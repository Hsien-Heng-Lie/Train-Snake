Snakey - How to Play

Objective

Eat as much food as possible and avoid colliding with walls or the snake's own body.

Controls

  • Arrow keys - Use the arrow keys to control the snake's direction: up, down, left, or right.

Rules

  1. The game starts with a snake consisting of a single segment on a bordered game board.
  2. The snake moves continuously in the direction specified by the arrow keys.
  3. The snake can wrap around the screen edges and appear on the opposite side.
  4. Food items are randomly placed on the game board.
  5. When the snake eats food, it grows longer by adding a segment to its tail.
  6. The game ends if the snake collides with the walls or its own body.

Scoring

The player earns points for each food item consumed. The score increases with each successful consumption.

Game Over

The game is over when the snake collides with the walls or its own body. The final score is displayed, and the player can choose to restart the game.
